What is Digital Identity?

Your Online Self

Your digital identity is the sum of all traces you leave online.

Digital Footprint

Every click, like, and share contributes to your digital presence.

Personal Data

Includes emails, social profiles, and online accounts.

"Your digital identity is the reflection of your actions online."

Your digital identity extends beyond social media profiles. It includes:

  • Browser history and cookies
  • Online shopping patterns
  • Digital communications
  • App usage and preferences

Digital Risks

Phishing

Deceptive attempts to steal your information.

Phishing Tactics

  • Fake emails and websites
  • Urgent action requests
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers

Scams

Fraudulent schemes to deceive and exploit.

Common Scams

  • Romance scams
  • Investment fraud
  • Tech support scams

Identity Theft

Unauthorized use of personal information.

Warning Signs

  • Unauthorized transactions
  • Missing mail or emails
  • Unknown accounts
